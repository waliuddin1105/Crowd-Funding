# Troubleshooting Guide

## Overview

This guide helps you resolve common issues you may encounter while using our crowdfunding platform. If these solutions don't resolve your problem, contact our support team.

## Account Issues

### Cannot Log In

**Problem**: Login fails with incorrect credentials

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Your Credentials**
   - Verify email address is correct
   - Check caps lock is off
   - Ensure no extra spaces
   - Try copying/pasting password

2. **Reset Password**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
   - Enter registered email
   - Check inbox for reset link (also check spam)
   - Link expires in 24 hours
   - Create new strong password

3. **Account Locked**
   - After 5 failed login attempts, account temporarily locked
   - Wait 30 minutes and try again
   - Or reset password immediately

4. **Email Not Verified**
   - Check for verification email (including spam folder)
   - Resend verification email from login page
   - Verify email before logging in

5. **Browser Issues**
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Try incognito/private mode
   - Try different browser
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily

**Still Can't Login?**
Contact: support@crowdfundingplatform.com with your registered email

---

### Account Verification Problems

**Problem**: Cannot verify identity or account

**Solutions:**

1. **ID Photo Issues**
   - Ensure photo is clear and well-lit
   - All corners of ID must be visible
   - No glare or shadows
   - Text must be readable
   - Take photo on plain background

2. **Selfie Verification Fails**
   - Hold ID next to your face
   - Ensure your face is clearly visible
   - Good lighting required
   - No sunglasses or face coverings
   - Camera must be steady

3. **Phone Verification Issues**
   - Enter phone number with country code
   - Format: +92XXXXXXXXXX (no spaces or dashes)
   - Check SMS isn't blocked
   - Try "Resend Code" if not received
   - Wait 60 seconds between attempts

4. **Email Verification Link Not Working**
   - Check link hasn't expired (valid 24 hours)
   - Copy entire URL into browser
   - Request new verification email
   - Check spam/junk folder

**Need Help?**
Email: verification@crowdfundingplatform.com

---

### Cannot Access Account

**Problem**: Logged out or cannot access features

**Solutions:**

1. **Session Expired**
   - Log in again
   - Enable "Remember Me" for longer sessions

2. **Account Suspended**

3. **Browser Not Supported**

4. **Cookies Disabled**
   - Enable cookies in browser settings
   - Allow cookies for our website

**Account Compromised?**
Email immediately: security@crowdfundingplatform.com

## Payment Issues

### Payment Failed

**Problem**: Donation or payment won't process

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Payment Details**
   - Verify card number is correct
   - Check expiration date is valid
   - Ensure CVV/CVC is correct
   - Confirm billing address matches

2. **Insufficient Funds**
   - Check account balance
   - Ensure sufficient funds available
   - Account for platform and processing fees

3. **Bank Declined Transaction**
   - Contact your bank
   - They may have flagged as suspicious
   - Confirm transaction with bank
   - Try again after confirmation

4. **Daily Limit Reached**
   - Check if you've exceeded daily spending limit
   - Wait until next day
   - Or increase limit with your bank

5. **Card Expired**
   - Check expiration date
   - Use valid, current card
   - Update payment method

6. **International Card Issues**
   - Ensure international transactions enabled
   - Some banks block international payments by default
   - Contact bank to enable

7. **3D Secure Failed**
   - Ensure receiving OTP from bank
   - Check SMS and email
   - Contact bank if not receiving OTP
   - Verify phone number with bank is current

8. **Try Alternative Payment Method**
   - Use different card
   - Try PayPal
   - Use mobile wallet (EasyPaisa/JazzCash)

**For Mobile Wallets (EasyPaisa/JazzCash):**
- Ensure sufficient balance
- Verify account is active
- Check MPIN is correct
- Ensure network connection stable

**Payment Stuck on Processing?**
- Wait 15 minutes
- Don't retry (may cause duplicate charge)
- Check email for confirmation
- Contact support if still processing after 1 hour

### Not Receiving Payment Confirmation

**Problem**: Payment went through but no confirmation email

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Spam Folder**
   - Look in junk/spam
   - Add our email to safe senders
   - Check all email folders

2. **Wait a Few Minutes**
   - Confirmation may be delayed
   - Usually arrives within 5 minutes
   - Check account donation history

3. **Verify Payment Processed**
   - Check bank/card statement
   - Check payment method account
   - Look for charge from our platform

4. **Incorrect Email**
   - Verify email in account settings
   - Update if incorrect
   - Request receipt resend

5. **Download from Account**
   - Log into account
   - Go to "My Donations"
   - Download receipt

**Still No Confirmation?**
Email: payments@crowdfundingplatform.com with:
- Transaction date and time
- Amount
- Payment method
- Last 4 digits of card (if applicable)

### Duplicate Charges

**Problem**: Charged twice for same donation

**Solutions:**

1. **Check if Authorization Hold**
   - One may be pending authorization
   - Should drop off in 24-72 hours
   - Only one will actually process

2. **Verify Both Processed**
   - Check if both show as completed
   - Look at transaction IDs (should be different)

3. **Contact Support Immediately**
   - Email: refunds@crowdfundingplatform.com
   - Include both transaction IDs
   - Provide screenshots of charges
   - We'll refund duplicate within 3-5 days

**Prevention:**
- Don't click "Pay" multiple times
- Wait for confirmation before retrying
- Check for processing indicator

### Cannot Withdraw Funds (Campaign Creators)

**Problem**: Unable to withdraw raised funds

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Campaign Status**
   - Campaign must be approved
   - Minimum threshold may apply
   - Verify milestones reached

2. **Bank Account Verification**
   - Ensure bank account verified
   - IBAN must be correct
   - Account name must match ID

3. **Documentation Required**
   - May need to provide receipts
   - Proof of fund usage might be required
   - Submit requested documents promptly

4. **Campaign Under Review**
   - Check for emails from verification team
   - Respond to any inquiries
   - Investigation may temporarily freeze funds

5. **Pending Period**
   - First withdrawal may have waiting period
   - Security measure for new accounts
   - Usually 7-14 days

**Withdrawal Timeline:**
- Request: Processed in 1-2 days
- Bank transfer: 3-5 business days
- Total: Up to 7 business days

**Need Urgent Withdrawal?**
Email: payments@crowdfundingplatform.com explaining urgency

## Campaign Issues

2. **Campaign Suspended or Removed**

4. **Try Different Search Terms**

5. **Check URL Directly**

6. **Browser Cache**
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try incognito mode
   - Try different browser

5. **Browser Issues**
   - Try different browser
   - Update browser to latest version
   - Disable browser extensions

6. **Clear Browser Cache**
   - Clear cache and cookies
   - Restart browser
   - Try again

7. **Image Quality**
   - Ensure images are clear
   - Good lighting
   - All text readable
   - All corners visible

## Technical Issues

### Page Not Loading

**Problem**: Website pages won't load or display errors

**Solutions:**

1. **Check Internet Connection**
   - Verify internet is working
   - Try loading other websites
   - Restart router if needed
   - Try different network

2. **Clear Browser Cache**
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: Options > Privacy > Clear Data
   - Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
   - Edge: Settings > Privacy > Choose what to clear

3. **Try Incognito/Private Mode**
   - Rules out browser extension issues
   - Bypasses cached data
   - If works in incognito: Clear cache in normal mode

4. **Update Browser**
   - Use latest version of browser
   - Update through browser settings
   - Restart browser after update

5. **Disable Extensions**
   - Ad blockers may interfere
   - Privacy extensions may block features
   - Temporarily disable and test

6. **Try Different Browser**
   - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge all supported
   - Mobile browsers also work

7. **Check Platform Status**
   - Visit status page (if available)
   - Check social media for announcements
   - May be temporary maintenance

**Error Code Shown?**
Note the error code and contact: tech@crowdfundingplatform.com

## Security Concerns

### Suspicious Activity on Account

**Problem**: Unusual activity you didn't authorize

**Solutions:**

**Immediate Actions:**

1. **Change Password Immediately**
   - Use strong, unique password
   - Different from other sites
   - Mix of upper/lower/numbers/symbols

2. **Log Out All Sessions**
   - Account Settings > Security
   - "Log Out All Devices"
   - Forces log out everywhere

3. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**
   - Account Settings > Security
   - Turn on MFA
   - Adds extra protection layer

4. **Review Account Activity**
   - Check recent logins
   - Review recent donations
   - Check campaign changes
   - Note any unauthorized actions

5. **Contact Support**
   - Email: security@crowdfundingplatform.com
   - Mark as URGENT
   - Explain what you've noticed
   - Provide details of suspicious activity

**What to Include in Report:**
- Unauthorized donations
- Login locations you didn't use
- Campaign changes you didn't make
- New bank details you didn't add
- Messages sent from your account

**We Will:**
- Investigate immediately
- Secure your account
- Reverse unauthorized actions if possible
- Provide guidance on next steps

### Phishing Emails

**Problem**: Suspicious emails claiming to be from us

**How to Identify:**

**Red Flags:**
- Asking for password or full card number
- Threatening account closure
- Urgent action required immediately
- Poor grammar or spelling
- Suspicious links or attachments
- From non-official email address

**Our Official Emails:**
- @crowdfundingplatform.com only
- We NEVER ask for passwords via email
- We NEVER ask for full card numbers
- We don't send unsolicited attachments

**What to Do:**
1. **Don't Click Links**
2. **Don't Download Attachments**
3. **Don't Provide Information**
4. **Forward to**: phishing@crowdfundingplatform.com
5. **Delete Original Email**

**Verify Legitimacy:**
- Log into your account directly (don't use email link)
- Check for notifications in account
- Contact support to verify
