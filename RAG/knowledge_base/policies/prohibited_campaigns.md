# Prohibited Campaigns

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## Overview

To maintain the integrity, legality, and trustworthiness of our crowdfunding platform, certain types of campaigns are strictly prohibited. This document outlines categories and activities that are not permitted on our platform.

Creating prohibited campaigns may result in immediate rejection, account suspension, or permanent ban, and may be reported to authorities if illegal activities are involved.

## Illegal Activities

### Strictly Prohibited
Any campaign that:
- Violates local, national, or international laws
- Facilitates criminal activity
- Promotes illegal goods or services
- Circumvents legal regulations
- Involves money laundering
- Supports terrorism or terrorist organizations
- Facilitates tax evasion or fraud

### Examples Include
- Purchasing illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Weapons without proper licensing
- Counterfeit goods or fake documents
- Illegal gambling operations
- Human trafficking or smuggling
- Piracy or copyright infringement
- Identity theft services
- Hacking services or tools

**Consequence**: Immediate campaign rejection, account termination, and reporting to law enforcement.

## Financial Instruments and Investments

### Not Allowed
Campaigns that involve:
- Offering securities or equity stakes
- Investment opportunities with promised returns
- Profit-sharing arrangements
- Cryptocurrency schemes or ICOs (Initial Coin Offerings)
- Multi-level marketing (MLM) or pyramid schemes
- Get-rich-quick schemes
- Forex trading funds
- Binary options trading
- Ponzi or pyramid schemes
- Unregistered financial products

### Why Prohibited
- These require regulatory approval and licensing
- Fall under securities regulations
- Create legal and financial risks for platform and donors
- Not structured as charitable donations

**Alternative**: Seek proper investment platforms or register with financial regulatory authorities.

## Deceptive or Fraudulent Campaigns

### Strictly Prohibited
- Campaigns with false or misleading information
- Fake medical conditions or emergencies
- Forged or falsified documentation
- Stolen identities
- Impersonating others
- Fabricated stories or situations
- Misrepresenting fund usage
- Duplicate campaigns (same cause, multiple accounts)
- Exaggerated or dishonest claims

### Warning Signs We Monitor
- Inconsistent stories across documents
- Stock photos claimed as personal
- Copied content from other campaigns
- Unable to verify identity or documents
- History of fraudulent activity
- Multiple campaigns for same purpose

**Consequence**: Immediate rejection, full refunds to donors, permanent account ban, potential legal action.

## Harmful or Dangerous Content

### Prohibited Activities
Campaigns that promote, support, or facilitate:
- Violence or threats of violence
- Self-harm or suicide
- Terrorism or hate crimes
- Child abuse or exploitation
- Animal cruelty or abuse
- Dangerous stunts or activities
- Weapons of mass destruction
- Explosives or bomb-making materials
- Harmful drugs or substances
- Vigilante justice

### Hate-Related Content
No campaigns involving:
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Racism, sexism, homophobia, or other bigotry
- Religious persecution
- Ethnic or cultural hatred
- Extremist ideologies
- Harassment or bullying campaigns

**Consequence**: Immediate removal, account termination, reporting to authorities.

## Adult and Sexual Content

### Not Permitted
- Pornography or sexually explicit content
- Adult entertainment businesses
- Escort services or prostitution
- Sex toys or adult products
- Sexually explicit performances
- "Sugar daddy/baby" arrangements
- Sexual services of any kind
- Dating services with sexual focus

### Exception
- Legitimate sexual health and education campaigns (with prior approval)
- LGBTQ+ rights and awareness (non-sexual focus)
- Reproductive health services (educational/medical)

Must be appropriate, educational, and comply with all guidelines.

**Consequence**: Campaign rejection, potential account suspension.

## Intoxicating Substances

### Prohibited
- Illegal drugs or controlled substances
- Drug paraphernalia
- Marijuana/cannabis (even where legal locally, due to international platform)
- Synthetic drugs or "legal highs"
- Prescription drugs without license
- Alcohol for personal consumption
- Tobacco products

### Limited Exceptions
May allow with strict documentation:
- Legitimate pharmaceutical research (with institutional approval)
- Medical marijuana in approved medical facilities (with license)
- Alcohol for licensed businesses (wedding venues, restaurants) with proper permits

**Consequence**: Campaign rejection unless exception criteria met with full documentation.

## Weapons and Dangerous Items

### Not Allowed
- Firearms and ammunition
- Explosives and fireworks
- Military weapons or equipment
- Knives and bladed weapons (except for culinary/professional use with documentation)
- Chemical or biological weapons
- Weapon modification parts
- Body armor
- Surveillance equipment for illegal purposes
- Lockpicking tools for illegal use

### Possible Exceptions
- Licensed security businesses
- Registered collectors (with proper documentation)
- Culinary knives for restaurants/chefs
- Film/theater props (with verification)

Must provide all licensing and legal documentation.

**Consequence**: Rejection, potential reporting to authorities.

## Political Campaigns

### Generally Prohibited
- Political campaign funding (candidates running for office)
- Political party funding
- Lobbying activities
- Political action committees (PACs)
- Election-related advertising

### Why Restricted
- Political campaigns have specific regulatory requirements
- Campaign finance laws vary by jurisdiction
- Requires specialized compliance and reporting
- Not structured as charitable crowdfunding

### Possible Exceptions
- Non-partisan voter registration drives
- Civic education initiatives
- Community organizing (non-partisan)
- Social issue advocacy (not candidate-specific)

**Alternative**: Use platforms specifically designed for political fundraising with proper regulatory compliance.

## Gambling and Games of Chance

### Not Permitted
- Lottery or raffle campaigns
- Casino or gambling businesses
- Sports betting operations
- Online gambling platforms
- Games of chance for money
- Sweepstakes without proper licensing
- "50/50" draws or similar

### Exception
- Licensed charitable raffles (with documentation)
- Legal gaming businesses in approved jurisdictions (with full licensing)

**Consequence**: Campaign rejection unless properly licensed.

## Multi-Level Marketing (MLM)

### Prohibited
- MLM business startup funding
- Network marketing recruitment
- Pyramid scheme participation
- Commission-based recruitment structures
- "Downline" building campaigns
- "Business opportunity" schemes with recruitment focus

### Why Not Allowed
- Often predatory business models
- High failure rates
- Regulatory concerns
- Not charitable in nature
- Misleading income claims common

**Consequence**: Campaign rejection, account review.

## Personal Debts and Loans

### Generally Not Allowed
- Paying off credit card debt
- Personal loan repayment
- Mortgage payments (unless facing foreclosure with documentation)
- Car loans or financing
- Tax debt (unless exceptional circumstances)
- Court fines or penalties
- Gambling debts
- Debt consolidation

### Why Restricted
- Not emergencies or genuine hardship causes
- Personal financial mismanagement
- Better addressed through financial counseling or bankruptcy

### Possible Exceptions
- Debt from medical emergencies (with medical documentation)
- Business debt from legitimate hardship (COVID-19 impact, natural disaster)
- Preventing homelessness (foreclosure with proof)

**Consequence**: Usually rejected unless exceptional circumstances with documentation.

## Prohibited Business Categories

### Not Acceptable
- Payday lending or high-interest loan services
- Check cashing services
- Pawn shops
- Debt collection agencies
- Bail bonds services
- Work-from-home schemes with unclear purpose
- "Make money online" courses without credentials
- Unproven weight-loss products or services
- Miracle cure or supplement companies
- Pseudo-scientific health products

### Why Prohibited
- Regulatory concerns
- High potential for consumer harm
- Often predatory practices
- Lack of evidence-based benefits

**Consequence**: Campaign rejection.

## Inappropriate Personal Campaigns

### Not Permitted
- Vacations or leisure travel (non-charitable)
- Wedding funding (unless cultural/financial hardship)
- "Send me to college" without demonstrated need
- Shopping sprees or luxury items
- Entertainment systems or gaming consoles
- Cosmetic surgery (non-medical)
- Party or celebration funding
- Car purchases (unless disability accommodation)

### Why Generally Declined
- Not genuine hardship or need
- More appropriate for personal savings
- Reduces credibility of legitimate campaigns
- Platform meant for meaningful causes

### Exceptions Considered
- Cultural/religious ceremonies with demonstrated financial hardship
- Education with clear financial need and documentation
- Medical-related procedures with doctor's recommendation

**Consequence**: Likely rejection unless exceptional circumstances.

## Prohibited Practices

### Manipulation and Fraud
- Creating multiple accounts for same campaign
- Fake donations to boost credibility
- Manipulating donor lists or amounts
- Hiding negative information
- Deleting critical comments inappropriately
- Plagiarizing others' stories
- Using stock photos as personal photos

### Spam and Abuse
- Unsolicited mass messaging to donors
- Spamming social media with campaign links
- Aggressive or harassing fundraising tactics
- Creating campaigns just to spam

### Terms Violation
- Circumventing platform fees
- Directing donors to off-platform payments
- Sharing user data inappropriately
- Violating intellectual property rights

**Consequence**: Campaign removal, account suspension or termination.

## Intellectual Property Violations

### Not Allowed
- Campaigns using others' copyrighted content without permission
- Trademark infringement
- Selling counterfeit goods
- Unauthorized use of celebrity names/images
- Pirated software, media, or content
- Patent infringement

### You Must
- Own rights to all content (photos, videos, text)
- Obtain permissions for third-party content
- Respect trademarks and copyrights
- Remove content if DMCA claim filed

**Consequence**: Campaign suspended until violation corrected; repeated violations result in termination.

## Special Restrictions

### Minors
- Campaign creators must be 18+
- Campaigns for minors require parent/guardian as creator
- Minors cannot receive funds directly
- Child exploitation strictly prohibited

### Animals
- Animal welfare campaigns allowed
- Animal breeding businesses prohibited
- Animal fighting or cruelty prohibited
- Exotic/illegal animal purchases prohibited

### International Campaigns
- Must comply with laws in both creator's country and Pakistan
- Sanctioned countries prohibited
- International money transfer restrictions apply
- Currency and banking regulations must be followed

## Reporting Prohibited Campaigns

### How to Report
If you encounter a prohibited campaign:

**Report Button**
- Click "Report Campaign" on campaign page
- Select reason for report
- Provide additional details
- Submit anonymously or with your name

**Contact Support**
- Email: abuse@crowdfundingplatform.com
- Include campaign URL
- Explain violation
- Provide evidence if available

### What Happens After Reporting
- Investigation within 24-48 hours
- Campaign may be suspended during review
- Creator notified if violation found
- Campaign removed if prohibited
- Serious violations reported to authorities
- You'll receive outcome notification (if you provided contact info)

## Consequences of Violations

### First-Time Minor Violations
- Warning issued
- Campaign rejected or suspended
- Opportunity to revise (if applicable)
- Education on policy

### Serious or Repeated Violations
- Permanent campaign removal
- Account suspension or termination
- Funds refunded to donors (if fraud)
- Ban from creating future campaigns
- Legal action (for illegal activities)
- Reporting to law enforcement (if necessary)

### Appeals
- You can appeal if you believe rejection was in error
- Provide additional information or documentation
- Appeal reviewed by senior team
- Decision communicated within 7-10 business days

## Best Practices for Compliance

### Before Creating Campaign
- Review this policy thoroughly
- Ensure your cause is legitimate and allowed
- Gather all necessary documentation
- Be truthful and transparent
- If unsure, contact support before submitting

### While Campaign is Active
- Keep information updated and accurate
- Respond to questions honestly
- Report any changes in circumstances
- Don't engage in prohibited practices
- Maintain respectful communication

### If Uncertain
**Contact Us**: compliance@crowdfundingplatform.com

We're happy to review your campaign idea before submission to confirm eligibility.
