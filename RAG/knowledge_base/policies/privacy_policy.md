# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our crowdfunding platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and respect.

By using our platform, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect information that identifies you as an individual, including:

**Account Registration**
- Full name

- Phone number

- Location (city, country)
- Profile picture (optional)

**Identity Verification (Campaign Creators)**

- Tax identification number (if applicable)

**Campaign Information**
- Campaign title and description
- Category and funding goal
- Supporting documentation (medical records, bills, certificates)

**Donation Information**
- Donation amount
- Payment method used
- Billing information
- Transaction history

### Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

**Device Information**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Screen resolution

**Usage Information**
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Campaigns viewed
- Search queries
- Click patterns
- Referral sources

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies**
- Session cookies (essential for platform functionality)
- Preference cookies (remember your settings)
- Analytics cookies (understand usage patterns)
- Marketing cookies (with your consent)

### Payment Information
**Payment Card Details**
- We never store complete card numbers

**Mobile Wallet Information**
- EasyPaisa/JazzCash mobile numbers (for transaction processing)

**PayPal Information**
- PayPal email or transaction ID
- No access to PayPal account credentials

## Data Security

### Security Measures
We implement comprehensive security to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards**
- **Encryption**: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- **Data Encryption**: Sensitive data encrypted at rest
- **Secure Servers**: Data stored on secure, access-controlled servers
- **Firewalls**: Network security and intrusion detection
- **Tokenization**: Payment card data tokenized, never stored

**Access Controls**
- **Role-Based Access**: Employees access only necessary data
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
- **Audit Logs**: Track all access to personal information
- **Limited Access**: Only authorized personnel access user data

**Organizational Measures**
- **Employee Training**: Regular security and privacy training
- **Confidentiality Agreements**: All employees sign NDAs
- **Security Audits**: Regular internal and external security assessments
- **Incident Response**: Procedures for handling security breaches

**Payment Security**
- **PCI-DSS Compliance**: Level 1 certified payment processing
- **No Card Storage**: Complete card details never stored
- **Secure Gateways**: All payments through certified providers
- **3D Secure**: Extra authentication layer for card payments

### Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach:
- We will investigate immediately
- Affected users notified within 72 hours
- Authorities informed as required by law
- Remedial actions taken promptly
- Transparent communication about the breach

## Cookies and Tracking

### What Are Cookies?
Small text files stored on your device that help us provide better service.

### Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (Always Active)
- Required for platform to function
- Session management and authentication
- Security and fraud prevention
- Cannot be disabled

**Preference Cookies** (Optional)
- Remember your settings and preferences
- Language selection
- Display preferences
- Can be disabled in settings

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional)
- Understand how you use our platform
- Identify popular features
- Measure performance
- Improve user experience
- Can be disabled in settings

**Marketing Cookies** (Optional - Requires Consent)
- Personalized campaign recommendations
- Targeted advertising (if enabled)
- Social media integration
- Can be disabled in settings

### Managing Cookies
Control cookies through:
- **Cookie Settings**: Available in footer of every page
- **Browser Settings**: Configure browser to block or delete cookies
- **Opt-Out Tools**: Use browser extensions for enhanced privacy

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

### Third-Party Cookies
Third-party services may set cookies:
- Google Analytics (usage tracking)
- Payment processors (transaction processing)
- Social media platforms (if you use social login)

Review their privacy policies for details.
