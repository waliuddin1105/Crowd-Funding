# Donor FAQs

## Making Donations

### What is the minimum donation amount?
The minimum donation is PKR 100. There is no maximum limit - you can donate as much as you'd like to support a cause.

### Can I donate to multiple campaigns?
Yes! You can support as many campaigns as you wish. Each donation is processed separately and you'll receive individual receipts for each contribution.

### How do I make a donation?
1. Browse or search for a campaign
2. Click "Donate Now" on the campaign page
3. Enter your donation amount
4. Choose your payment method
5. Complete the secure payment process
6. Receive instant confirmation and receipt

### What payment methods can I use?
We accept:
- **Credit/Debit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- **PayPal** (International and local)
- **EasyPaisa** (Mobile wallet and shops)
- **JazzCash** (Mobile wallet and agents)

### Can I make recurring donations?
Yes, you can set up monthly recurring donations to support ongoing causes. You can modify or cancel recurring donations anytime from your account dashboard.

### Can I donate in foreign currency?
International donors can donate in USD, EUR, or GBP through PayPal or international cards. The amount is automatically converted to PKR, and the exchange rate is displayed before you confirm.

### Will I receive a receipt?
Yes, you'll receive an email receipt immediately after your donation is processed. You can also download receipts anytime from your donation history.

### Can I get a tax deduction for my donation?
Tax deductibility depends on the campaign type and your local tax laws. Contact support for a tax receipt if the campaign qualifies as a charitable contribution in your jurisdiction.

## Donation Security

### Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use:
- Industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
- PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processing
- 3D Secure authentication for cards
- Tokenization (we never store complete card details)
- Real-time fraud monitoring

### What if my payment fails?
Common reasons for payment failure and solutions:
- **Insufficient funds**: Check your account balance
- **Incorrect details**: Verify card information
- **Bank decline**: Contact your bank
- **Expired card**: Use a valid card
- **Network timeout**: Try again after a few minutes

If problems persist, contact our support team.

### Can someone else see my donation?
By default, your name appears on the campaign page as a supporter (amount visible). You can choose to donate anonymously to keep your identity private, though the campaign creator will still see your name for receipt purposes.

### What happens to my donation if the campaign doesn't reach its goal?
Your donation goes to the campaign creator regardless of whether the goal is met. Our platform uses a "flexible funding" model where creators receive all funds raised. If you're concerned, check if the campaign has a refund policy specified.

## Tracking Your Donations

### How can I track campaigns I've supported?
View all your donations in your account dashboard under "My Donations." You'll see:
- Campaign names and dates
- Donation amounts
- Campaign progress updates
- Receipts and transaction details

### Will I get updates about the campaign I donated to?
Yes! Campaign creators can post updates visible to all supporters. You'll receive email notifications when campaigns you've donated to post new updates (you can adjust notification settings).

### Can I communicate with campaign creators?
You can leave public comments on campaign pages. For private inquiries, use the "Contact Creator" button to send a direct message.

### What if the campaign creator isn't providing updates?
If a campaign you supported hasn't been updated in a while, you can:
- Message the creator directly
- Report the concern to our support team
- Request a review of the campaign's status

## Refunds & Disputes

### Can I get a refund?
Generally, donations are non-refundable as they're voluntary contributions. However, refunds may be granted in cases of:
- Proven fraud or misrepresentation
- Technical errors (duplicate charges)
- Campaign cancellation before fund disbursement
- Unauthorized transactions

### How do I request a refund?
Contact support within 30 days with:
- Transaction ID
- Reason for refund request
- Supporting evidence (if claiming fraud)

Our team will review and respond within 5-7 business days.

### How long do refunds take?
Once approved, refunds are processed within:
- Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
- PayPal: 3-5 business days
- EasyPaisa/JazzCash: 3-5 business days

Timing may vary based on your bank or payment provider.

### What if I was charged twice by accident?
Contact support immediately with both transaction IDs. We'll verify the duplicate charge and process a refund for the extra charge within 3-5 business days.

### Can I dispute a charge with my bank?
While you can initiate a chargeback through your bank, we encourage contacting us first. Bank chargebacks can take longer and may affect your account standing. We're committed to resolving issues quickly and fairly.

## Anonymous Donations

### How do I donate anonymously?
Check the "Donate Anonymously" box during checkout. Your name won't appear on the campaign page or in the donor list.

### Will the campaign creator know who I am?
Campaign creators receive your name and email for receipt purposes, even for anonymous donations. However, your information won't be displayed publicly.

### Can I send a message anonymously?
Public comments display your username. For completely anonymous communication, create a separate account or contact support to relay a message.

### Does anonymous donation affect my receipt?
No, you'll still receive a complete receipt with all transaction details. The anonymity only affects public visibility on the campaign page.

## International Donations

### Can I donate from outside Pakistan?
Yes! We welcome international donors. Use PayPal or international credit/debit cards to contribute from anywhere in the world.

### What currency will I be charged in?
- If you use PayPal: You can choose your currency; PayPal handles conversion
- If you use an international card: You're charged in PKR; your bank handles conversion
- Exchange rates are displayed before final confirmation

### Are there additional fees for international donations?
Standard platform and payment processing fees apply. Your bank may charge foreign transaction fees - check with your card issuer.

### Will currency conversion rates be fair?
We use real-time market rates. PayPal's rates are competitive and displayed upfront. For card payments, your bank's exchange rate applies.

## Campaign Selection

### How do I know which campaigns are legitimate?
Look for these trust indicators:
- **Verified Badge**: Campaign passed our admin review
- **Complete Documentation**: Photos, videos, and detailed story
- **Regular Updates**: Active creator engagement
- **Donor Activity**: Other supporters have contributed
- **Recent Activity**: Recent donations and updates

### What if I can't decide which campaign to support?
Consider these factors:
- **Urgency**: Medical emergencies vs. long-term projects
- **Progress**: Help campaigns close to their goal
- **Location**: Support your local community
- **Category**: Focus on causes you're passionate about
- **Story**: Connect with campaigns that resonate with you

### Can I donate to campaigns that haven't been verified yet?
No. Only verified campaigns that have passed our admin review process are visible and available for donations.

### How do I know my donation will make an impact?
- Read the campaign's detailed description and funding breakdown
- Check for regular updates from the creator
- Look at comments from other donors
- Review photos and documentation
- Contact the creator with questions before donating

## Supporting Campaign Creators

### Should I share campaigns I've donated to?
Yes! Sharing campaigns on social media helps creators reach their goals faster. Most campaign pages have built-in sharing buttons for easy posting.

### Can I leave a message with my donation?
Yes, you can include a public message of support when you donate. This appears on the campaign page (unless you donate anonymously) and encourages others to contribute.

### What if I want to donate but can't afford much?
Every contribution matters, no matter the size. Even small donations of PKR 100-500 add up and show campaign creators they have community support.

### Can I volunteer or help in non-monetary ways?
While our platform focuses on financial contributions, you can help by:
- Sharing campaigns with your network
- Leaving encouraging comments
- Providing advice or expertise to creators
- Connecting creators with relevant resources

## Technical Issues

### My payment went through but I didn't receive confirmation
Check your spam/junk folder first. If still not found, contact support with your:
- Transaction date and time
- Payment method used
- Amount donated
- Campaign name

We'll verify the transaction and resend your receipt.

### The donation page isn't loading
Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Use a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Can I complete a donation on my mobile phone?
Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-responsive. You can browse campaigns and donate securely from any smartphone or tablet.

### What if I accidentally donated to the wrong campaign?
Contact support immediately (within 24 hours if possible). While donations are generally non-refundable, we may be able to help if funds haven't been disbursed yet.

## Responsible Giving

### How much should I donate?
Donate an amount you're comfortable with. There's no pressure to give beyond your means. Even small contributions help when combined with others' support.

### Should I research the campaign before donating?
Yes, we encourage due diligence:
- Read the full campaign story
- Review uploaded documentation
- Check creator's profile and history
- Read comments from other donors
- Ask questions if anything is unclear

### What if a campaign seems suspicious?
Trust your instincts. If something doesn't seem right:
- Report the campaign using "Report" button
- Contact support with your concerns
- Don't donate until you feel confident
- Look for verified campaigns with strong documentation

### Can I set donation limits for myself?
While we don't have built-in spending limits, you can:
- Set personal monthly giving budgets
- Use payment methods with spending limits
- Track your donations in your dashboard
- Set up recurring donations for consistent giving
