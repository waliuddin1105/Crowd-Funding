# Payment FAQs

## General Payment Questions

### What payment methods do you accept?
We support multiple payment options to ensure accessibility:
- **Credit/Debit Cards** (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Maestro)
- **PayPal** (International and domestic)
- **EasyPaisa** (Mobile wallet, shop, and ATM payments)
- **JazzCash** (Mobile wallet and agent payments)

### Which payment method is fastest?
All payment methods process instantly:
- **Immediate confirmation**: Cards, PayPal, mobile wallets
- **Instant receipt**: Emailed immediately upon successful payment
- **Campaign credit**: Donation appears on campaign within minutes

### Which payment method has the lowest fees?
**EasyPaisa and JazzCash** have the lowest processing fees at 1.5%. However, all methods have a standard 5% platform fee.

Fee comparison:
- EasyPaisa/JazzCash: 5% + 1.5% = 6.5% total
- Cards: 5% + 2.5% = 7.5% total (plus PKR 10 fixed)
- PayPal: 5% + 3.4% = 8.4% total (plus fixed fee)

### Can I use multiple payment methods for one donation?
No, each donation must be completed with a single payment method. However, you can make multiple separate donations using different payment methods.

### Is my payment information stored?
No, we never store complete card details. We use:
- **Tokenization**: Sensitive data converted to secure tokens
- **PCI-DSS Compliance**: Industry-standard security
- **No card storage**: Full card numbers never saved
- **Encrypted transmission**: All data encrypted in transit

## Card Payments

### My card payment was declined. Why?
Common reasons for card declines:
- **Insufficient funds**: Check your account balance
- **Incorrect details**: Verify card number, expiry, CVV
- **Bank security**: Your bank may have flagged the transaction
- **Expired card**: Use a valid, current card
- **International restrictions**: Some banks block international transactions
- **Daily limit exceeded**: You've reached your card's daily spending limit

**Solution**: Contact your bank or try a different card.

### What is 3D Secure verification?
3D Secure (Verified by Visa, Mastercard SecureCode) adds an extra security layer:
- You receive an OTP (One-Time Password) from your bank via SMS
- Enter the OTP to confirm the transaction
- Protects against unauthorized card use
- Required for most online transactions

If you don't receive an OTP, contact your bank.

### Can I use international credit cards?
Yes! International cards from any country are accepted. Currency conversion happens automatically, and the exchange rate is displayed before you confirm payment.

### What if I'm charged twice?
If you see duplicate charges:
1. Check if both transactions show as completed (one may be a pending authorization)
2. Wait 24 hours for pending charges to clear
3. If both charges process, contact support immediately with both transaction IDs
4. We'll verify and refund the duplicate within 3-5 business days

### Are there transaction limits for card payments?
- **Minimum**: PKR 100
- **Maximum per transaction**: PKR 500,000
- **Daily limit**: PKR 1,000,000 per card
- Your bank may impose additional limits

### Can I save my card for future donations?
Currently, we don't offer card storage for security reasons. You'll need to enter card details for each donation. This protects your financial information.

## EasyPaisa Payments

### How do I pay with EasyPaisa mobile account?
1. Select EasyPaisa as payment method
2. Enter your EasyPaisa registered mobile number
3. Dial *786# on your mobile
4. Select "Payments" option
5. Enter the transaction ID provided
6. Enter your EasyPaisa PIN
7. Confirm payment

You'll receive instant SMS confirmation.

### Can I pay at EasyPaisa shops?
Yes! Shop/ATM payment process:
1. Select "EasyPaisa Shop/ATM" option
2. System generates a voucher code
3. Visit any EasyPaisa shop or ATM within 24 hours
4. Provide voucher code to agent
5. Pay in cash or via mobile account

### What if my EasyPaisa payment fails?
Common issues:
- **Insufficient balance**: Load your EasyPaisa account
- **Incorrect PIN**: Verify your EasyPaisa PIN
- **Account blocked**: Contact EasyPaisa helpline 3737
- **Network issue**: Try again after a few minutes
- **Transaction limit**: Check your daily limit

### What are EasyPaisa transaction limits?
- **Minimum**: PKR 100
- **Maximum per transaction**: PKR 200,000
- **Daily limit**: PKR 500,000
- **Monthly limit**: As per your EasyPaisa account type

### I paid at EasyPaisa shop but didn't receive confirmation
If you don't receive confirmation within 15 minutes:
1. Check your SMS for transaction receipt
2. Verify payment with shop agent (get receipt)
3. Contact support with:
   - Voucher code
   - Shop location and time
   - Agent receipt/transaction ID
4. We'll verify and credit your donation

### Can I use EasyPaisa without a mobile account?
Yes, use the "Over The Counter" (OTC) option:
- Generate payment voucher on our platform
- Visit any EasyPaisa shop with cash
- Provide voucher code to agent
- Pay in cash
- No EasyPaisa account required

## Payment Security

### How secure are online payments?
We implement multiple security layers:

- **PCI-DSS Level 1**: Highest payment security certification
- **3D Secure**: Extra authentication for card payments
- **Fraud Detection**: Real-time transaction monitoring
- **Tokenization**: Sensitive data converted to secure tokens

### What is PCI-DSS compliance?
Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) is an internationally recognized security framework that ensures secure handling of card information. Our Level 1 certification means we meet the highest security requirements.

### Can someone hack my payment information?
We minimize this risk through:
- Industry-standard encryption
- No storage of complete card details
- Secure payment gateways
- Regular security audits
- Multi-factor authentication
- Real-time fraud monitoring

If you suspect unauthorized access, contact support immediately.

## Troubleshooting

### My payment is stuck on "Processing"
Usually resolves within 15 minutes. If longer:
1. Don't retry payment (may result in duplicate charge)
2. Check your bank/wallet account (was payment deducted?)
3. Check email for confirmation
4. Wait 1 hour, then contact support with:
   - Payment method used
   - Approximate time
   - Amount

### I was charged but donation doesn't show on campaign
Don't worry! This happens when:
- **Pending authorization**: Will process soon (15-30 minutes)
- **Email delay**: Check spam folder for receipt
- **System lag**: Campaign updates within an hour

If not resolved in 2 hours, contact support with transaction ID.

### Payment failed but I was charged
This is usually a pending authorization that will reverse automatically within 24-72 hours. If charge remains after 3 days, contact support with:
- Transaction ID or screenshot
- Date and time
- Amount charged
- Payment method

### Can I retry payment after failure?
Yes, you can attempt payment again immediately. Ensure you've resolved the issue that caused the initial failure (insufficient funds, incorrect details, etc.).
