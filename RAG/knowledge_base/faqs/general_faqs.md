# General FAQs

## Platform Basics

### What is this crowdfunding platform?
Our platform is a secure online service that connects people who need financial help with generous donors willing to support verified causes. We facilitate fundraising for medical emergencies, education, disaster relief, community projects, and other legitimate needs.

### How does the platform work?
Campaign creators submit their fundraising campaigns with proper documentation. Our admin team verifies each campaign before it goes live. Once approved, donors can browse campaigns and contribute securely through multiple payment methods. Funds are transferred to campaign creators after verification milestones.

### Is the platform free to use?
Creating an account and browsing campaigns is completely free. However, we charge a 5% platform fee on successful donations to maintain and improve our services. Additionally, payment processing fees apply based on the chosen payment method.

### Who can use this platform?
Anyone 18 years or older can create an account. Campaign creators must provide valid identification and documentation. Donors can be from anywhere in the world, though some payment methods may be region-specific.

### Is my information secure?
Yes, we implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, PCI-DSS compliance, and secure data storage. We never store complete card details, and all transactions are encrypted.

## Account Questions

### Do I need an account to donate?
While you can browse campaigns without an account, you need to create a free account to make donations, track your donation history, and receive receipts.

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. The link expires in 24 hours for security.

### Can I change my email address?
Yes, you can update your email address in Account Settings. You'll need to verify the new email before the change takes effect.

### Can I delete my account?
Yes, contact our support team to request account deletion. Note that you must complete or cancel any active campaigns before deletion, and some information may be retained for legal compliance.

## Campaign Discovery

### How do I find campaigns to support?
Browse campaigns by category on the homepage, use the search function to find specific causes, or filter by location, funding progress, or urgency. All displayed campaigns have been verified by our admin team.

### What does "Verified Campaign" mean?
A verified campaign has passed our rigorous review process. Our admin team has authenticated the documentation, confirmed the creator's identity, and validated the legitimacy of the cause.

### How are campaigns categorized?
Campaigns are organized into categories including:
- Medical Emergencies
- Education & Scholarships
- Disaster Relief
- Community Projects
- Business & Startups
- Personal Emergencies
- Religious & Cultural Events

### Can I search for campaigns in my area?
Yes, use the location filter to find campaigns in your city or region. This helps you support causes in your local community.

## Trust & Safety

### How do you prevent fraud?
We employ multiple layers of fraud prevention:
- Mandatory identity verification for all campaign creators
- Document authentication and background checks
- AI-powered fraud detection systems
- Continuous monitoring of active campaigns
- User reporting mechanisms
- Admin review of all campaigns before approval

### What if I suspect a fraudulent campaign?
Report it immediately using the "Report Campaign" button on the campaign page. Our team will investigate promptly and take appropriate action, including suspending the campaign if fraud is confirmed.

### Can I get a refund if a campaign is fraudulent?
Yes, if a campaign is confirmed as fraudulent, we will process full refunds to all donors. Refer to our Refund Policy for complete details.

### Are campaign creators background-checked?
Yes, we conduct basic background checks including identity verification, document authentication, and cross-referencing with fraud databases. High-value campaigns may undergo additional scrutiny.

## Technical Support

### What browsers are supported?
Our platform works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for optimal security and performance.

### Is there a mobile app?
Currently, our platform is fully accessible through mobile web browsers. A dedicated mobile app is planned for future release.

### I'm experiencing technical issues. What should I do?
Check our Troubleshooting Guide first. If the issue persists, contact support through:
- Email: support@crowdfundingplatform.com
- Live Chat (available 9 AM - 9 PM PKT)
- Help Center ticket system

### Can I donate from outside Pakistan?
Yes! We accept international donations through PayPal and credit/debit cards. Currency conversion is handled automatically, and rates are displayed before you confirm your donation.

## Fees & Costs

### What fees do you charge?
- **Platform Fee**: 5% of the donation amount
- **Payment Processing Fees**: Vary by method
  - Credit/Debit Cards: 2.5% + PKR 10
  - PayPal: 3.4% + fixed fee
  - EasyPaisa: 1.5%
  - JazzCash: 1.5%

### Why do you charge fees?
Platform fees help us maintain secure infrastructure, conduct thorough campaign verification, provide customer support, and continuously improve our services. Payment processing fees are charged by third-party payment providers.

### Can campaign creators cover the fees?
Campaign creators can factor fees into their target amount, but we don't currently offer an option for creators to cover donor fees at checkout.

### Are there any hidden fees?
No. All fees are clearly displayed before you complete your donation. What you see is what you pay.

## Privacy & Data

### How is my personal information used?
We use your information solely to process donations, communicate important updates, and improve our services. We never sell your data to third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for complete details.

### Can I donate anonymously?
Yes, you can choose to make your donation anonymously. Your name won't appear on the campaign page, but you'll still receive receipts for your records.

### Will I receive marketing emails?
You'll receive transactional emails (receipts, campaign updates) automatically. Marketing emails are optional and can be disabled in your notification preferences.

### How long is my data retained?
We retain account data as long as your account is active. Financial transaction records are kept for 7 years for legal compliance. You can request data deletion by contacting support.
